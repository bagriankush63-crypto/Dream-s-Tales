*MERE JAANE KE BAAD*

— Poet: Ameya

Mai tumhe yaad zaroor aungi 
Tumhare khayalo ka thoda hissa to bankar hi jaungi
Jab jab rat ko tum karvat liya kroge 
yad krna har karb tumhara mai  suna krti thi 
Har karj tumhara mai apna samjha krti thi
Har savere ki phli dhup jab pdegi tumpr 
Toh smjh jana maii mere khwabo se jagane ayi hu tumhe
Har sham jb thak kar tum ghr aaooge 
Angan me bhaithe hawa ka jhonka mehsus kroge 
Smjh jana sukoon dene aayi hu tumhe 
Har raat jab tumhe nind na aye 
Meri tasveere dekha karna tum 
Vo tumhe dekh muskuraayengi 
kuch to kehna chahengii
Ankahe alfaz smjh jana tum 
Sirhane me sab gam baha dena 
Samjh jana tumhare ansuu mitane aayi hu 
Har marz ki tumhare dawa banne ayi hu
Tumhara hissa to m bankar hi jaungii 
Jab us ag ke bich jalungi maii 
Rakh bnungii maii ,dil jalega to usse  khusbhu teri ayegi.
Jo bhi ho 
Mai tumhe yaad zrur aungi
Tumhara hissa toh bankar reh jaungi
